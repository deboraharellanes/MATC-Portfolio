Context, Audience and Purpose
AJ lives in Lancaster California and works as an electrical engineer. He has been running an evening/weekends home remodeling business out of his house. All his customers are by word of mouth. No business cards, brochures, or a website. My internship assignment was to design a website for him to help him attract new customers. He provided me with the basic information of photos of his past work and the list of jobs he is able to do. The purpose of the website is to showcase his work and provide a way for new customers to find him. My audience is anyone in his area of southern California who would like their home remodeled.

Design Process
I created the logo AJ Remodels using Canva design software. I used a graphic of a tan paint slab of paint in a brush stroke with black text for good contrast. For repetition, The logo in the left corner and repeated in center alignment in the footer. The color scheme is tan and brown and I used light to dark, 90 degree, linear gradients in the background, header, and footer creating contrast and repetition. Black text contrasts against the background. The navigation bar and bulleted list of skills is left aligned creating reptition. On the second page I created a table for the photos which takes up the entire page. The last page has all the same repetitive elements with a centered email submission box.

Strengths
It is strait forward, in large readable sans-serif text with relevent information, listing job skills and photos of past work and an email submission form. No clutter and extra stuff to drowned out the main points and distract.
